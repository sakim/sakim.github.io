Privacy Policy

Skip Off Days Alarm & Check Day

This Privacy Policy explains how the following apps handle your information: Skip Off Days Alarm and Check Day.

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Skip Off Days Alarm An alarm app that skips selected off days and custom days off automatically.
Check Day A simple daily routing tracker built around quick check-ins.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

These apps store the information needed to provide their core features on your device. Depending on the app and features you use, this may include alarm settings, labels, repeat schedules, selected country or region, custom off days, and off-day skip settings.

For Skip Off Days Alarm, this mainly includes alarm settings and off-day skip preferences. For Check Day, this mainly includes your check-in data and related viewing or tracking preferences stored on your device.

We do not require you to create an account to use these apps.

2. Off-Day Information

Off-day or holiday information may be fetched from public Google Calendar holiday data for the selected country or region. Custom off days you add manually are stored on your device.

3. Advertising

These apps may display ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may process device identifiers, diagnostic information, approximate location, and other data as described in Google's own privacy materials in order to serve and measure ads.

Where required, we show a consent form through Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP). On iOS, you may also see Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before personalized advertising is used.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob for advertising.
  • Google UMP for consent messaging and privacy choices.
  • Public Google Calendar holiday sources for off-day information.

Third-party services process data under their own policies and terms.

5. Your Choices

  • You can change or delete alarms, labels, and custom off days inside the apps.
  • You can review privacy choices through the in-app privacy options screen when available.
  • You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can remove locally stored data by deleting the app from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

These apps are not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted at this page with the updated date shown above.
